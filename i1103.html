origin and date unknown
language undetermined
One of the ancient forms of written documents were chisled inscriptions.
When Moses received the Tablets of the Law written by ''GOD's finger'' they would have looked similar to the inscription on this stone.
Many of the letters found here are similar to ancient writings including Hebrew. The stone bears the look of great age however its actual age and language have not been determined.
