Collections > Books > Chronological & Geographical Family Guide to the Holy Bible  < Previous | Next >

No date, ca. 19th century
A beautifully and highly ornamented engraved map of the Holy Land. Measuring 29 1/4'' x 25'', the map is filled with interesting features, including scriptual references, day by day chronology of events in Jesus time, dates of composition of the various N.T. books with their writers, and events in Bible history which occurred at various family lines which eventually lead to Christ's birth.
The map folds up neatly into attached covers.
