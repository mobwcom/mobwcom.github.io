M u s e u m I n f o r m a t i o n

Twenty-eight years ago, Paul Do started this collection all over Europe and for many years searched and gathered. Today, the Museum of Biblical World is comprised of hundreds of religious historical Bible related antiques including Bibles, artwork, and more. Although he has donated some artifacts to other museums, his whole collection has not been exhibited to the public. This website serves the purpose of showcasing and cataloging the collection.
