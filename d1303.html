Beautiful Engraving of Jesus, Parable of the Wedding Feast
ca. 1840
In this parable a guest shows up for a wedding feast without wearing proper garments to fit the occassion. On seeing this the king questions him then has him bound and tossed outside. This represented the religious hypocrites of Jesus day who fail to meet GOD's requirements of righteousness and are cast out from further service to Him.
