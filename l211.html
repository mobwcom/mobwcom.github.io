circa: 1st Century CE
Lovely child's glass bracelet dated around the first century. Just as toady's parents like to make their children look pretty with accessories. The parents of the first century also wished to do the same for their children. Jewelry of the precious metal (such as gold) were too expensive for many, so a glass bracelet such as this would beautify their children for a reasonable cost.
